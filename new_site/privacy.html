Privacy Policy

How Smart VR Lab protects your data

This notice explains what personal data we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have when you use our website, VR CMS, or headset applications.

Last updated: January 2026

1. Who we are

Smart VR Lab provides a VR content management platform, headset applications, and related services. We are the data controller for the personal data described in this policy unless stated otherwise.

2. Data we collect

  • Account and contact data: name, email address, company, role, and login credentials.
  • Usage data: actions taken in the CMS, device identifiers, session logs, and performance metrics.
  • Content data: media files, titles, descriptions, and metadata you upload or create.
  • Support data: messages, feedback, and issue details shared with our support team.
  • Website data: cookies or similar technologies for analytics and improving the site experience.

3. How we use your data

  • Provide and secure the VR CMS, headset apps, and related services.
  • Set up and manage user accounts, roles, and device permissions.
  • Process uploads, transcode media, and deliver content to authorized devices.
  • Monitor performance, troubleshoot issues, and improve product features.
  • Communicate updates, onboarding guidance, and service notifications.
  • Comply with legal obligations and enforce our Terms & Conditions.

4. Legal bases

We process personal data based on contract performance (to deliver the services), legitimate interests (to secure and improve the platform), consent where required (e.g., certain cookies), and legal obligations.

5. How we share data

We may share data with trusted processors such as hosting providers, analytics tools, and customer support platforms under data processing agreements. We do not sell personal data. We may disclose data to comply with law, protect our rights, or in connection with a merger or acquisition.

6. Data retention

We keep personal data for as long as needed to deliver the services and meet legal requirements. Account data is retained while your subscription is active. Backups and logs are retained for a limited time for security and recovery purposes.

7. Security

We apply technical and organizational measures including access controls, encryption in transit, monitoring, and least-privilege principles. No system is fully secure, so please use strong passwords and keep devices up to date.

8. Your rights

  • Access, correct, or delete your personal data.
  • Object to or restrict certain processing.
  • Port data you provided to us.
  • Withdraw consent where processing is based on consent.
  • Lodge a complaint with your supervisory authority.

To exercise your rights, contact us at info@smartvrlab.nl. We may need to verify your identity before fulfilling your request.

9. International transfers

If data is transferred outside the EEA or the UK, we use appropriate safeguards such as Standard Contractual Clauses or equivalent protections required by law.

10. Contact

Questions about this policy or your personal data? Email info@smartvrlab.nl. For commercial enquiries, please use the contact form.
