Case study

AR with Buddy

An innovative Dutch hospital wanted to make kids’ stays less scary. They created Buddy, a friendly bird mascot, and we brought it to life in AR so children (3-9) can interact, feel at ease, and have fun.

Buddy checks in with feelings, plays games, does tricks, and reads stories and jokes—turning hospital time into a playful experience.

  • Augmented Reality app for Android
  • Dynamic content for ages 3-6 and 6-9
  • Gives young patients a Buddy in hospital
  • Features games, stories, and jokes
