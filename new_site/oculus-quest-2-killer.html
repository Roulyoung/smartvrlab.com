Why choose the Oculus Quest 2 Killer for VR (or why not) A competitor’s analysis.

adminsvr · February 22, 2021
← Back to blog

Oculus Quest 2 is an awesome piece of hardware. It?s got a high resolution, good tracking quality and it?s rocking one of the most powerful mobile CPUs out there for AR and VR functions. It?s cheap relative to almost any other headset, which is why it has flown off shelves. But it is about to be heavily tested by its competition.

Meet the Qiyu 3 challenger

iQIYI is a Chinese media company designing XR devices, with several successful products in Asia. Launching the Qiyu 3 is a direct attack on the Quest 2, and it will ship with the Snapdragon XR2 just like Quest 2.

Cooling and performance headroom

Same processor doesn?t mean same power. John Carmack has noted Quest 2 is undervolted and underclocked for cooling reasons. Qiyu 3?s upgraded cooling system could squeeze more performance from the XR2.

Dual displays and clarity

Qiyu 3 adds dual displays for better IPD adjustment and clarity, reaching 2160?2160 per eye?about 43% more pixels than Quest 2. Its tracking aims for millimetre-level positioning accuracy and low latency.

Outlook for Quest 2 competition

Quest 2 now faces stiff competition. With headsets offering more power, better displays, and potentially lower prices, the VR market is heating up. iQIYI has targeted Quest 2, and other challengers are sure to follow.
