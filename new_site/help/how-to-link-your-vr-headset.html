How to link your VR headset

Time: ~5 minutes Device: Meta Quest with Smart VR Lab app

Use these steps to add your VR device to Smart VR Lab, enter the 5-digit code from the web app, name it, and authorize access so your team can publish content.

Jump to steps Back to helpdesk
What you need
  • Access to app.smartvrlab.nl
  • Meta Quest headset with internet
  • Smart VR Lab app installed on the headset
Outcome

Your headset appears in Devices with a green Authorized status and is ready to receive published content.

You can always make all published content unavailable to each device registered by setting the Status column to a red cross.

Step 1 - Log in to your account

Log into your Smart VR Lab account on app.smartvrlab.nl.

Step 2 - Navigate to Devices

Navigate to the tab Devices and click on the button Add Device.

Step 3 - Get 5 Digit Code

When you press the Add Device button, a 5 digit code will appear on the web platform. Go to your VR Device and start the Smart VR Lab app. To get the Smart VR Lab app on your Meta Quest you can get it here. In the VR device you will be asked to fill this 5 digit code.

If the code you filled in is accepted, you have successfully linked your VR Headset to Smart VR Lab, congratulations!

Step 4 - Give Your Device a Name

As soon as you enter the code to link the VR headset a new tab will pop-up named: New Device and it will show you the device data. You're able to register it under a device name (Headset 1 for example) and assign an owner (e.g. the person in charge of the device). If you hit Save then your device will be linked to the server.

Step 5 - What does Authorize do?

Make sure the status of your device has a green checkmark in the Devices tab. If it doesn't, press the red cross. A green check mark should appear. In case you unchecked the Authorize checkmark in the Add device window, the VR device will not be able to access any content.

You can always make all published content unavailable to each device registered by setting the Status column to a red cross.

Step 6 - Publish Content

Your device is now active and you're ready to publish content! If you already uploaded content, you can publish by following these steps: How to publish content. If you didn't upload content, please upload VR Videos and VR Images first.
