How to link your VR headset

Time: ~5 minutes Device: Meta Quest with Smart VR Lab app

Use these steps to add your VR device to Smart VR Lab, enter the 5-digit code from the web app, name it, and authorize access so your team can publish content.

Jump to steps Back to helpdesk
What you need
  • Access to app.smartvrlab.nl
  • Meta Quest headset with internet
  • Smart VR Lab app installed on the headset
Outcome

Your headset appears in Devices with a green Authorized status and is ready to receive published content.

Step 1 - Log in

Open app.smartvrlab.nl in your browser and sign in to Smart VR Lab.

Step 2 - Go to Devices

In the top navigation of the web app, select Devices and click Add Device.

Step 3 - Get the 5-digit code

After you press Add Device, a unique 5-digit code appears. Keep this page open.

On your Meta Quest, open the Smart VR Lab app. If you need the app, download it from Meta here. Enter the 5-digit code when prompted.

When the code is accepted, the headset is paired with your account.

Step 4 - Name the device

Back in the Devices tab on the web app, a new device entry appears. Give it a clear name (for example, Headset 1) and assign an owner. Click Save.

Step 5 - Authorize

Check the status of the device. If you see a red cross, click it to authorize. A green checkmark means the headset can access content.

Step 6 - Publish content

When the device shows a green checkmark, you can publish content to it. If you have not uploaded media yet, start with your videos and 360 photos, then publish a playlist to this headset.
