Helpdesk Content / Specs

Supported filetypes

Time: 2 minutes Device: Web app

Use the formats below for best results when uploading videos, audio, and images to Smart VR Lab.

Jump to formats Back to helpdesk
What you need
  • Video files: MP4 (H.264/H.265)
  • Images: JPG or PNG (equirectangular for 360)
  • Audio: AAC in your MP4
Outcome

Uploads transcode reliably and play smoothly on headsets and web.

Videos

Format: MP4 (H.264 or H.265)

Resolution: Up to 4K recommended for 2D; use 5.7K/6K for high-quality 360.

Frame rate: 24-60 fps; keep source and export consistent.

Images

Format: JPG or PNG

Projection: Equirectangular for 360 images; 2:1 ratio (e.g., 7680x3840) preferred.

Audio

Codec: AAC within MP4

Channels: Stereo recommended; ensure levels are normalized to avoid clipping.

Tips for best results

Keep file names simple (letters, numbers, dashes). Avoid special characters.

Use consistent aspect ratios and test on a headset after upload to verify quality.
